The Great Breathing Styles
Sun Breathing
The first one to have ever been created. All other Breathing styles are derived from this. Has twelve forms.
Thunder Breathing
Mimics lightning. Involves utilizing blinding speeds and immensely fast attacks to overwhelm the enemy in an instant.
Flame Breathing
Mimics flame. Involves extremely powerful singular strikes which 'burn' the opponents. Has nine forms.
Water Breathing
Mimics water, specifically the flow, flexibility and adaptability of the liquid. Most common among Demon Slayers.
